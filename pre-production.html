#1

Developing The Idea

Asking Fundamental Questions

"Why Am I Doing This?"

"What will be the impact?"

"What response do I hope to elicit?"

Answering some of these can provide a direction as you develop your script

Key: Passion (Self) + Interest (Audience)

Deepen the story with Details

By Researching Your Topic

Creating the Content

1. Main Characters - Relatable & interesting

2. Goal - What main characters want to achieve

3. Obstacles - What prevents the characters from achieving the goals

*Empathy - Involve/engage the audience, help them to identify & feel for the characters

The 3-Act Structure

Rule:

Act Duration Description
First ~25% Setup
Second middle 50% Confrontation
Third final 25% Resolution
